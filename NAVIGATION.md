## Moving Between Layers

tiezerk connects outward to the broader UNEXUS ecosystem:

- **→ ashkharh**: Individual entities and characters inhabit the environments defined here. When a character is *in* the Quantum Forest, the physics come from tiezerk; the character comes from ashkharh.
- **→ nexusiam**: Play and development environments simulate or extend what tiezerk defines. nexusiam is where these environments are tested, played, and grown.
- **→ gubernaculum**: Governance and ethics structures apply to how these environments are used and shared.

See the `connections/` directory for detailed crossover protocols.

## Traversal Principles

- **Shadow before emergence** — approach new environments in draft state; don't over-finalize too soon.
- **One hertz** — pacing matters; let understanding accumulate at its own frequency.
- **The mountain, not the climber** — speak of the environments as vast and independent, not as settings for human-scale drama.
