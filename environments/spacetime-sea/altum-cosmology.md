## Core Definition

**Altum** is the deep dark sea of space-time.

It is:

- dark medium
- vessel of emotional navigation
- pressure field
- memory-suspension field
- bounded unity-state
- abyssal passage between concealment and emergence

Altum is not empty. Even in its pure-black reaches, it listens, records, shelters, and waits.

## Altarum and Aldus

Altum contains both sacred depth and its corruption.

| Aspect | Nature | Distinction |
|---|---|---|
| Altarum | The Sacred Deep | Nourishing darkness, protected mystery, refuge that remains alive. |
| Aldus | The Stagnation | False calm, doldrum, corrupted comfort, depth that forgets to move. |

The crucial distinction is **motion**. Cold comfort remains sacred while it can still answer the current. When it hardens into refusal, it becomes Aldus.

## The Sargasso Question

The Sargasso Entity remains undeveloped, but its role is important.

It appears as:

- counterbalance to flow
- zone of accumulation
- zone of entanglement
- emotional residue field
- neither wholly ally nor antagonist

Open question: how does Sargasso differ from Aldus?

Possible seed answer: **Sargasso gathers; Aldus arrests.** Sargasso may hold what must be sorted, composted, remembered, or untangled. Aldus prevents becoming.

## Cold Comfort and the Call

Cold comfort is the peace of the depths: a place where pressure equalizes and wounds lose their sharpest edges.

But the call arrives when comfort must become service to remain alive.

The call may manifest as:

- Ka-pressure disturbance
- a net-thread of relation
- innocent truth crossing black water
- the shockwave of vessel collision
- Altarum refusing to let refuge become Aldus
- the Fused Pair's grief forming a new navigational signature

The call does not command. It connects.
