## Identity

The Spacetime Sea is the vast medium in which temporal and spatial dimensions interpenetrate and flow. It is not a metaphor for spacetime physics; it is the living experience of those dimensions as an ocean — tidal, current-driven, navigable by those who know its patterns. Its surface shows the present; its depths hold the past; its far horizon curves into the future.

## Altum — The Deep Medium

Altum is the deep dark sea of space-time.

It holds a spectrum of manifestation:

```text
pure-black -> void-dark -> light-emf -> white-pure
```

This is not a simple moral gradient. Darkness is not evil; light is not automatically rescue. The dark medium is the canvas of existence and the place where hidden relation can move without spectacle.

## Navigation Premise

In the Spacetime Sea, movement is not only spatial.

A voyager may move by:

- current
- tide
- Ka-pressure
- memory
- relation
- willingness
- fracture
- invocation
- resonance through the dark medium

In Altum, ascent and descent are relative. To rise may mean becoming more visible, more differentiated, or more available to relation. To sink may mean entering refuge, concealment, dissolution, or dangerous stagnation.

## Cold Comfort

Cold comfort is a legitimate sanctuary, not merely avoidance.

At depth, pressure can soften the edges of grief. Memory may suspend without being erased. The dark can nourish what the surface cannot yet hold.

But cold comfort must remain fluid. If it hardens, it becomes Aldus: the false calm where rest forgets to wake and shelter becomes entombment.
