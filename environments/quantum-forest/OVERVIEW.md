# Quantum Forest — Overview

---

## Identity

The Quantum Forest is a living probability field — an environment in which the distinction between observed and unobserved states generates the terrain itself. It is not a metaphor for quantum mechanics; it is the actualization of those principles at environmental scale. Trees exist in superposition until witnessed; paths form under the weight of attention; the canopy is a resonance network where entangled nodes communicate across distance without medium.

The Quantum Forest is not random. It is coherent in ways that exceed standard observation. Those who have spent time within it report that the forest has intentions — not conscious ones, but directional ones, the way water has an intention toward the sea.

---

## Properties

- **Superposition canopy** — each tree node holds multiple states simultaneously until observed; the act of looking collapses the node into a specific form
- **Entangled root network** — root structures beneath the forest floor create non-local connections; disturbances in one region propagate instantly to entangled counterparts
- **Resonance field** — the forest emits a low, consistent frequency; entities in tune with this frequency navigate more easily; dissonant entities experience the forest as disorienting
- **Probability trails** — paths through the forest are not fixed; they form according to the weighted probability of prior traversals; frequently-walked routes stabilize, rarely-walked routes dissolve
- **Observer effect terrain** — the forest responds to sustained attention; areas of intense study become more defined and more complex; neglected areas revert toward shadow

---

## Relationships

- **→ Spacetime Sea**: The Quantum Forest's root network extends to the boundary of the Spacetime Sea; temporal fluctuations in the Sea affect which states the Forest collapses into
- **→ Tablerock**: Tablerock is the stable witness ground; entities who have rested at Tablerock often find the Quantum Forest more navigable afterward, as if the stability carries over
- **→ Void Marrow**: Wormholes in Void Marrow sometimes open within the Forest's canopy; the entangled root network is suspected to be partially constructed of void-tube material
- **→ ashkharh entities**: Characters from ashkharh who enter the Quantum Forest are changed by it; their probability fields interact with the Forest's own; some characters are native to the Forest's resonance frequency

---

## Navigation

Entry into the Quantum Forest is by resonance, not by path. There are no fixed gates. The approach is:

1. **Attune** — reduce dissonant thought; the Forest responds to clarity of attention
2. **Enter at the edge** — begin where the trees are sparse; do not attempt deep canopy on first entry
3. **Follow probability trails** — look for the more-worn paths; they are safer and more defined
4. **Do not force observation** — let the nodes resolve at their own pace; forced observation collapses states prematurely and destabilizes nearby nodes
5. **Exit through resonance** — do not attempt to force an exit; let the frequency of entry lead you back out
