## Resonance Rules — Seed Draft

1. Resonance precedes visible contact.
2. Repeated patterns strengthen field recognition.
3. A form may resonate with a place, a memory, a path, or another form.
4. Strong resonance may cause emergence without direct command.
5. Discordant resonance does not always destroy; sometimes it creates a new branch.

---

## Entanglement Rules — Seed Draft

1. Entanglement records relationship, not ownership.
2. Entangled growth may be mutual, asymmetrical, or delayed.
3. A branch can carry consequence from another branch without copying its form.
4. Distance alone does not break entanglement.
5. Forgetting may obscure entanglement without dissolving it.
