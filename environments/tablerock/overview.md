# Tablerock — Overview

---

## Identity

Tablerock is the oldest stable formation in the UNEXUS cosmology — a vast, flat geological structure of unknown origin that predates the current cosmic cycle. It exists in a region of spacetime where the Spacetime Sea's tides cannot reach and the Quantum Forest's probability fields do not collapse. Tablerock simply is. Its surface is flat and unbroken, extending farther than any entity has traversed. Its age is beyond calculation; its stability is absolute.

Entities come to Tablerock to rest, to orient themselves, and to remember what is constant. The rock does not speak, but those who sit on it long enough report a kind of clarity — as if the absence of fluctuation allows the self to become visible to itself. This is Tablerock's primary function in the UNEXUS world: it is the witness ground. Not a recorder, not a judge — a ground.

---

## Properties

- **Absolute stability** — Tablerock does not fluctuate, probability-collapse, or shift with temporal tides; it is the most stable surface in the known UNEXUS world
- **Vast horizontal extent** — the surface extends beyond explored range; no boundary has been found
- **Witness resonance** — the rock emits a deep, slow resonance (~0.01 Hz) that induces clarity of thought and reduced internal noise in entities that sit on it
- **Non-interactive surface** — Tablerock does not respond to observation or intervention; it is immune to observer effects and temporal manipulation
- **Geological record** — the surface layers contain compressed traces of everything that has rested or passed above it, encoded in mineral structure; these records are readable only by specific entities
- **Foundation depth** — Tablerock's foundations extend deep into the Spacetime Sea's historical strata; at depth, the rock is as old as the Sea itself

---

## Relationships

- **→ Spacetime Sea**: Tablerock sits above the Sea's tidal range; its foundations extend into the deep strata; the Sea flows around it but does not erode it
- **→ Quantum Forest**: The Quantum Forest's probability fields do not penetrate Tablerock; entities emerging from the Forest find Tablerock stabilizing — their superposed states collapse naturally here
- **→ Void Marrow**: No wormholes open on Tablerock's surface; void tubes pass beneath but do not intersect the surface layer; Tablerock is a void-neutral zone
- **→ ashkharh entities**: Many ashkharh entities make periodic pilgrimages to Tablerock; it is considered a site of resting and recalibration rather than a destination in its own right

---

## Navigation

Tablerock is approached from any direction at ground level. There are no gates or entry conditions. The approach:

1. **Arrive at the edge** — Tablerock's boundary is felt as a change in ground resonance; the ambient noise of the surrounding environments diminishes
2. **Step onto the surface** — no ritual or permission required; the rock accepts all who arrive
3. **Sit** — rest is the primary activity here; extended sitting deepens the witness resonance effect
4. **Depart when ready** — Tablerock does not hold entities; departure is as simple as standing and walking back to the edge

There are no known hazard regions on Tablerock's surface. The greatest risk is remaining too long and losing motivation to engage with the more dynamic environments — Tablerock's stability can become its own kind of gravity.
