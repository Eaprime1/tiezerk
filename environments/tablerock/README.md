# Tablerock

*The ancient witness ground — stable where all else flows.*

---

## Identity

Tablerock is the oldest stable formation in the UNEXUS world — a vast, flat geological structure that sits above the fluctuations of the Spacetime Sea and outside the probability fields of the Quantum Forest. It does not change. It does not react. It witnesses. Entities come to Tablerock to rest, to orient, to remember what is constant. The rock itself holds a record of everything that has passed above it — not as data, but as presence.
