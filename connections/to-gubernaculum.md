# tiezerk → gubernaculum

*How tiezerk environments relate to governance and ethics structures.*

---

## The Relationship

gubernaculum is the governance and ethics layer of the UNEXUS ecosystem — the structures that define how the world is used, shared, and protected. tiezerk defines the universal environments. gubernaculum defines the rules and ethics of how those environments are engaged with.

The relationship is one of stewardship: tiezerk provides the terrain; gubernaculum provides the custodianship. Neither controls the other. gubernaculum does not override tiezerk's environmental physics; tiezerk does not override gubernaculum's ethical structures.

---

## Governance Principles for tiezerk Environments

The following principles, drawn from gubernaculum, apply to the use of tiezerk environments:

1. **Non-exploitation** — tiezerk environments are not resources to be extracted; they are environments to be navigated with respect for their own properties
2. **Witness accountability** — Witnessmarks create accountability; entities who witness a transition are responsible for the accuracy of their witnessing
3. **Emergence protection** — shadow-state concepts and entities are protected; forcing emergence before readiness violates gubernaculum principles
4. **Shared stewardship** — no single entity owns a tiezerk environment; all entities who interact with it share responsibility for its integrity

---

## Cross-Repo Governance

When a tiezerk environment property is used in another repository (ashkharh, nexusiam), gubernaculum principles apply:

- Properties should be referenced, not copied and diverged
- Changes to canonical properties require a Witnessmark and acknowledgment from the tiezerk community
- Disputes about canonical properties are resolved through gubernaculum processes
