# tiezerk → nexusiam

*How tiezerk environments connect to play and development spaces.*

---

## The Relationship

tiezerk defines the universal environments as they are — fundamental, elemental, independent. nexusiam is where these environments are engaged with playfully, experimentally, and developmentally. nexusiam is the testing ground, the sandbox, the place where new interactions are explored before they are ready to be declared in tiezerk's canonical layer.

Nothing that happens in nexusiam changes tiezerk directly. But discoveries in nexusiam can seed new canonical properties in tiezerk, once those discoveries have been witnessed and stabilized.

---

## Direction of Flow

```
tiezerk (canonical) → nexusiam (play/dev) → discovery → witnessed → tiezerk (updated)
```

- tiezerk definitions flow into nexusiam as the starting physics
- nexusiam experiments, plays, and extends
- Significant discoveries are extracted from nexusiam and proposed for canonical inclusion in tiezerk
- Proposals require a Witnessmark before becoming canonical

---

## What nexusiam Can Do with tiezerk Environments

- **Simulate** — run scenarios in which the tiezerk environment physics apply
- **Extend** — add properties or behaviors for play purposes (labeled as nexusiam-local, not canonical)
- **Break** — explore what happens when Universal Constants are violated; document results without importing them as canonical changes
- **Seed** — propose new canonical properties based on play discoveries; submit via PR with Witnessmark

---

## What nexusiam Cannot Do

- Directly modify tiezerk canonical files
- Declare new Universal Constants without going through the witnessed-emergence process
- Represent nexusiam-local extensions as tiezerk canonical properties
